Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not require accounts. We collect minimal technical data required to serve files, such as IP addresses (for rate limiting) and file metadata.

2. Cookies

We use local storage only to remember your upload history on your device. We do not use tracking cookies.

3. File Retention

Files are stored on secure cloud servers. We maintain a reference to them in our database. Files can be deleted using the unique delete key provided at upload.
